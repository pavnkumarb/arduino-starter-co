## 3. Pricing Strategy

### 3.1 Fee Structure Analysis

| Fee Component | Rate | Amount (on $59 list price) |

| Amazon referral fee (Electronics category) | 15% | $8.85 |
| FBA pick & pack fee (small standard, ~0.9 lb) | flat | ~$3.22 |

| **Total Amazon fees** | | **~$12.15** |
| Net proceeds at $59 | | **$46.85** |

*FBA rates based on 2026 small standard size (product dimensions estimated: 9×6×3 in, ~0.9 lb shipped weight). Verify against actual packaging dimensions before final pricing.*

### 3.2 Recommended Amazon Price: **$59**

**Rationale for parity pricing (not premium):**

- Our Shopify DTC price is $59. Amazon parity prevents arbitrage friction and avoids customer confusion when comparing channels.
- At $59 we land exactly at the top of the "mid" price tier ($45–70), above Elegoo's $44–65 range, and well below Arduino Official's $93–110. This is the defensible positioning.
- Amazon customers expect Amazon-parity pricing. A premium creates negative reviews ("cheaper on their own site").
- We accept lower per-unit margin on Amazon vs. DTC ($46.85 vs. $59.00 net) in exchange for Amazon traffic, Prime badge, and review velocity — all of which feed DTC long-term brand awareness.

### 3.3 Price vs. Competitors at $59

| Competitor | Price | Our Position |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Elegoo Super Starter ($44.99) | $45 | $14 premium — must be justified by differentiators |
| Elegoo Most Complete ($64.99) | $65 | $6 below — better value signal at equal or superior experience |
| Arduino Official K000007 | $93–110 | $34–51 below — "same quality, not paying for the brand name" |
| SparkFun SIK v4.1 | $45–65 | Parity — our tutorial story is stronger |

### 3.4 Promotional Pricing Guidance

- **Launch (first 90 days):** No discounts. Build at full price to establish price anchor and avoid training customers to wait for sales.
- **Prime Day:** 20% off coupon ($47.20). This positions us below Elegoo Most Complete during the sale window.
- **Black Friday / Cyber Monday:** 25% off ($44.25) — matches Elegoo's regular price, framing us as premium at same cost.
- **Never discount below $44** — that crosses into Elegoo's budget-tier positioning and breaks our brand.

## 4. FBA vs. FBM Recommendation

### Recommendation: **FBA (Fulfilled by Amazon)**

**Analysis:**

| Factor | FBA | FBM |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Prime badge | ✅ Yes — estimated 80%+ of buyers filter for Prime | ❌ No (unless seller-fulfilled Prime, which requires 1-day shipping capability) |
| Conversion rate impact | +15–25% over equivalent non-Prime listings (Amazon internal data) | Baseline |
| Per-unit cost | ~$3.22 pick/pack + storage | ~$4–8 (actual shipping to buyer, varies) |
| Margin vs. DTC | ~$46.85 net at $59 | ~$51–55 net at $59 (depending on actual shipping) |
| Inventory risk | Amazon holds and ships; possible stranded inventory | Full control, no stranded inventory risk |
| Returns | Amazon handles return process | We handle returns |
| Launch velocity | Higher — Prime filter + Buy Box advantage | Slower |
| Best for | Launch phase: volume, reviews, ranking | Scale phase: margin optimization |

**Decision basis:** At launch, we need review velocity above margin optimization. FBA's Prime badge is the single largest conversion lever available. The ~$4 margin advantage of FBM is not worth sacrificing Prime eligibility during the ranking build phase.

**Margin impact summary:**

| Channel | Revenue | Amazon Fees | COGS (est. $22) | Gross Margin | GM% |
|---------|---------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|-----|
| Shopify DTC | $59 | ~$2 (Shopify + payment) | $22 | $35 | 59% |
| Amazon FBA | $59 | ~$12.15 | $22 | $24.85 | 42% |
| Amazon FBM | $59 | ~$8.85 + ~$5 shipping | $22 | $23.15 | 39% |

*COGS estimate of $22 includes BOM, printed guide, component reference card, and packaging. Verify with operations.*

### 5.2 Vine Review Program

**Target:** 30 Vine reviews within 30 days of first shipment.

**Why 30 units:**
- Amazon Vine maximum enrollment is 30 units for a new ASIN.
- The listing needs a minimum 4.0 average across ≥15 reviews to achieve Amazon's Choice eligibility.
- 30 Vine reviews at our expected quality provides the social proof foundation for paid traffic to convert.

**Cost:** $200 (flat Vine enrollment fee, one-time per ASIN).

**Timing:** Enroll immediately upon first FBA shipment arrival. Do not wait.

**Risk:** Vine reviewers are honest — a product that doesn't deliver on its promises will surface issues. Our kit must be fully tested before enrollment. If tutorial videos are not live, do not enroll.
