## Overview

Organic search is a compounding, low-CAC channel. Every article we publish earns traffic month after month without additional spend. The goal of this strategy is to own the top beginner Arduino search queries before our paid launch scales, so that organic acquisition is already working when the budget ramps.

This document covers:
1. Keyword research — 10+ terms, top 5 selected for the first five posts
2. Per-post briefs (keyword, secondary terms, title, meta, outline)
3. Publishing calendar (1 post/week pre-launch)
4. On-page SEO checklist
5. Internal linking map

The five full blog post drafts live alongside this file in `docs/blog/`.

## 4. On-Page SEO Checklist (Every Post)

- [ ] Primary keyword in H1 (exact or close variant)
- [ ] Primary keyword in meta description (within first 60 chars)
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words of body
- [ ] 2–3 secondary keywords used naturally in H2s or body
- [ ] Internal link to product page (at least once)
- [ ] Internal link to at least one other blog post (once published)
- [ ] CTA with product page link before and at end of post
- [ ] Word count 800–1,200 words
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text including keyword where natural
- [ ] URL slug matches primary keyword (hyphens, lowercase, no stop words)
- [ ] Brand voice: no "simple," "easy," "just," "obviously"
