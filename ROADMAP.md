# Arduino Starter Kit — Product Roadmap

**Company Goal:** Launch Validated Arduino Starter Kit
**Revenue Target:** $2M
**Target Audience:** Beginners (age 12+, students, hobbyists, educators)

---

## Phase 1 — Discovery & Validation (Weeks 1–6)

**Goal:** Understand the market, validate demand, and confirm product-market fit before committing to production.

### Milestones
- [ ] Competitive landscape analysis complete
- [ ] Primary target persona(s) defined and validated
- [ ] Pricing sensitivity research complete
- [ ] Kit contents wishlist from user interviews
- [ ] Supplier shortlist (5+ candidates) assembled

### Key Deliverables
- Competitive analysis report
- 2–3 validated user personas
- Component requirements brief
- Supplier research doc

### Owners
- UX Researcher: market research, persona development, user interviews
- Customer Success: competitive analysis, pricing research
- Product Owner: component requirements, supplier brief

---

## Phase 2 — Product Definition (Weeks 5–10)

**Goal:** Lock down the kit's exact contents, curriculum, and price point.

### Milestones
- [ ] Kit contents v1 finalized (BOM — Bill of Materials)
- [ ] 5-project beginner curriculum outline approved
- [ ] Packaging concept approved
- [ ] Price point and margin model validated
- [ ] Supplier selected and samples ordered

### Key Deliverables
- Bill of Materials (BOM) v1
- Curriculum outline with 5 beginner projects
- Packaging brief
- Unit economics model

### Owners
- Product Owner: BOM, curriculum outline, unit economics
- UX Researcher: usability validation of curriculum
- Engineer: technical review of component selection

---

**Goal:** Establish brand identity, design system, and core marketing assets.

**Goal:** Build the storefront and fulfillment pipeline so the kit can be sold online.

### Milestones
- [ ] E-commerce platform selected and configured (Shopify preferred)
- [ ] Product listing live (draft)
- [ ] Payment and shipping configured
- [ ] Order management workflow documented
- [ ] Analytics and tracking installed

### Key Deliverables
- Live Shopify store (staging)
- Product page with copy and images
- Fulfillment SOP
- Analytics dashboard

### Owners

- Product Owner: fulfillment requirements, SOP sign-off

---

## Phase 5 — Soft Launch & Validation (Weeks 15–20)

**Goal:** Sell first 100 units, collect feedback, and validate operational readiness.

### Milestones
- [ ] Pre-launch waitlist or beta buyer program (50+ sign-ups)
- [ ] First 100 units shipped
- [ ] NPS or satisfaction survey deployed
- [ ] At least 10 customer feedback interviews completed
- [ ] Critical bugs and friction points resolved

### Key Deliverables
- Beta program report
- Customer satisfaction baseline (NPS target: ≥40)
- Known issues log with priority fixes
- Revised product/curriculum v1.1

### Owners
- CMO: launch campaign, waitlist, influencer outreach
- Customer Success: feedback collection, support setup
- Engineer: bug fixes, store optimizations
- Product Owner: issue triage, v1.1 scope

---

## Phase 6 — Growth & Scale (Months 5–12)

**Goal:** Reach $2M revenue target through channel expansion, content flywheel, and product line growth.

### Milestones

### Key Deliverables
- Amazon storefront
- Educator outreach kit
- YouTube tutorial series (5 videos)
- Kit v2 spec
- Partner program landing page

### Owners
- CMO: Amazon listing, ads, affiliate program, YouTube
- Engineer: Amazon API integration, store scaling
- Customer Success: educator outreach, community building
- Product Owner: Kit v2 scope, roadmap iteration

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Supplier quality issues | Medium | Order samples before bulk; dual-source key components |
| Shipping cost overruns | Medium | Model unit economics with buffer; negotiate bulk rates |
| Low organic traffic | High | Invest in SEO + YouTube early; educator channel |
| Component shortage | Low-Medium | Build 60-day buffer inventory |
| Curriculum too complex for beginners | Medium | User test with target age group before print |
